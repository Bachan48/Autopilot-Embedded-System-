History of AES


1905 AD

  • 1908
    Anschuts Gyroscope was invented.

  • 1911
    Sperry Gyroscope was invented.

  • 1912
    First autopilot was developed by Sperry Corporation.

  • 1914
    Demonstrated by Lawrence Sperry with his hands away from the controls and visible to onlookers.

  • 1920
    The Standard Oil tanker J.A.Moffet became the first ship to use an autopilot.

  • 1931
    For the first time in history a mechanical autopilot is licensed to fly passengers and airmail. The Department of Commerce permites it to serve as copilot of a large Condor 18 passenger plane.

  • 1932
    Sperry Gyroscope Company developed the automatic pilot that Wiley Post would use in his first solo flight aroung the world.

  • 1933
    Further developments of the autopilots were performed, such as improved control algorithms and hydraulic servomechanics. Also, inclusion of additional instrumentation such as the radio-navigation made it possible to fly at night and during bad weather.
